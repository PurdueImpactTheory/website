Our Story

Rumination

The idea started between the original President and Treasurer who would discuss issues with the way physics was perceived in society. Most people they encountered pitied them having to pursue physics in college, seeming to believe it was just memorizing equations.

Brewing

It soon became evident that similar misunderstandings existed for all types of science majors, which sparked the concept for the PIT. After spreading the idea to some close friends and faculty, it caught on and became an organization of about ten members and two faculty at its conception.

Becoming official

The PIT was created in the fall of 2017 with a group of students ranging in backgrounds across nearly all science majors. Since their recognition as a Purdue student organization, members of the PIT have worked to make their presence more aware on campus and to the community.

Moving forward

The network of people surrounding their mission grows every month, and the ultimate dream is truly to make an impact on society and to lay the groundwork for some of the biggest movements yet to come for science.
