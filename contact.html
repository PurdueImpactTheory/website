Talk to us

In tackling its mission, Purdue Impact Theory engages in outreach events and collaborations that bring the world closer together. Have questions or want to get involved? Feel free to contact us at pit.purdue@gmail.com!

Collaborate

We collaborate with a variety of groups including Purdue University science departments, outreach offices, and high school educators. Sometimes we chat about ongoing projects for feedback, and other times we create outreach/education resources together!

Outreach

Every semester, we partake in Purdue community outreach events, our own on-campus events, or call-out events for students to learn about what we do. You can stay in the loop by visiting our Purdue page (BoilerLink).
