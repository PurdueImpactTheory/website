Talk to us

In tackling its mission, the Purdue Impact Theory aims to engage society through sponsorships and collaborations that help to bring the world together.
Feel free to contact us at pit.purdue@gmail.com for any questions you might have.

Collaborate

Collaboration efforts are both sought after and accepted by other organizations, on or off Purdue's campus.

Sponsorships

Some sponsorship benefits include the following (these benefits can fluctuate based on semester and specific partnership):

  • Receiving sponsor newsletters detailing what the organization is doing and how it is performing
  • Obtaining a contact point for feedback on the performance/direction of the organization
  • Seeing sponsor name on any official presentation or setup while in public, as well as on the PIT website and social media

We would be honored to partner with you or your company to help make an impact on society.
